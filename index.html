About Us

See Sa Rin is a wedding venue featuring three picturesque architectural buildings including “See Glasshouse”, “Sa Huean Thai” and “Rin Sanctuary”. Inspired by the phases of love, these venues are exquisitely the synopsis of the first love, the learning process, and the lifelong commitment.


Harmoniously blending Eastern and Western aesthetics, See Sa Rin seeks to perfectly accentuate your unprecedented love story. Beyond the remarkable buildings, a thousand-year-old olive tree stands a witness to your everlasting devotion on the day you will forever cherish....
